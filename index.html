Wizardry

The light & dark wizard have gathered their forces and prepair for a battle of wits. Both are willing to move mountains for the opertunity to best the other.

Your objective is to capture your opponents wizard, tower, or units with a clever use of spells and unit movements.

3 Realms

You are one of two omnipresent deities who are trying to develop the world so that those who serve you are more prosperous.

Your objective is to develop a civilization with the most influence and prosperity. To accomplish this, draft* (take a resource cards and then pass the rest of the cards in your hand to the other player) resource cards to set up the world. Then draft civilization cards and add them strategically to accomplish your objective.

Warring Brothers

The king is dead! Twin princes now vie for their place on the throne. The young princes have fought their whole lives for their father's approval and for the birthright to rule the kingdom. The king died without declaring an heir, and the two brothers are now in the midst of a civil war. Battle lines are drawn as each brother marshals his men to his cause.

In Warring Brothers, two players battle each other for supremacy on the battlefield. Your forces are represented by dice. Movement commands are selected in secret and revealed and resolved simultaneously.
